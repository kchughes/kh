A little about me

I am a Los Angeles native, current Chicago resident (okay, the suburbs, but it still counts!) pursuing a Master's in Technical Communication and Information Design at Illinois Institute of Technology.

I strongly believe that all forms of information should be accessible and easy to digest, no matter what the format. I apply this philosophy to everything I do. Creative problem-solving is one of my strengths, especially when the problem may be vocalized as simple as "This doesn't sound/look right" or "I'm not sure what's wrong."

With a goal-oriented approach to drafting design and content, I enjoy transforming I work very well collaboratively and I believe that design is a function of communication. Whatever the format, people absorb and react to information more positively when it is beautifully formatted, creatively displayed, and very well-written.

In addition to English, I am fluent in Spanish, proficient in French, and am learning Chinese and Japanese. When I'm not creating beautiful content and design for other people, I am reading, writing, or going on city hikes.

I've worked on many types of projects, either design, content, or a combination of the two (or both). Here is a sampling of the types of projects I've done:

  • Holiday cards digital )
  • Invitations digital print
  • PowerPoint presentation templates digital

I've been told that I'm also observant, thorough, creative, adaptable, and a team player. But I prefer thinking of myself as a all around professional.*
