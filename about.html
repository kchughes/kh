A little about me

I am a Los Angeles native, current Chicago resident (okay, the suburbs, but it still counts!) pursuing a Master's in Technical Communication and Information Design at Illinois Institute of Technology.

I strongly believe that information should be accessible and easy to digest, no matter what the format. I apply this philosophy to everything I do. Creative problem-solving is one of my strengths, especially when the problem may be vocalized as as simple as "This doesn't sound/look right" or "I'm not sure what's wrong."
